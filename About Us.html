About Us

Otto Building Ltd is a reputable construction and development company located in North London. With decades of expertise passed down through generations, our roots in the trade run deep, starting with knowledge and skills handed down from father to son. We specialize in premium residential renovations and commercial property maintenance, delivering excellence across North London and Hertfordshire. Our company takes pride in its legacy of quality and reliability. Every project we undertake is approached with meticulous care and precision, ensuring unmatched craftsmanship and attention to detail for our valued clients. Our dedication to building trust has resulted in a strong reputation, with most of our projects stemming from referrals and word-of-mouth recommendations. When you choose Otto Building Ltd, you partner directly with a skilled and dedicated team, benefiting from competitive supplier rates for high-quality kitchens, tiles, hardwood flooring, bi-fold doors, and bathroom fittings. We work closely with trusted architects and designers to create bespoke solutions tailored to your needs. By utilizing advanced tools and techniques, we ensure a cleaner and more efficient workspace, minimizing dust and disruption for our clients and team. Committed to sustainability, we strive to reduce energy consumption and waste on every project, putting both our clients and the environment at the heart of what we do.
